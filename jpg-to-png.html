What is JPG to PNG Conversion?

JPG to PNG conversion is the process of changing an image format from JPEG to PNG. JPG files are compressed and may lose quality, while PNG files are lossless and maintain better clarity. This tool allows you to convert JPG to PNG online free without losing quality.

Why Convert JPG to PNG?

PNG format provides higher quality and supports transparency. It is ideal for logos, graphics, and images that require clear backgrounds. Many designers and developers prefer PNG over JPG for better visual output.
