What is EMI Calculator?

EMI calculator is an online financial tool that helps you calculate monthly EMI for loans such as home loan, car loan, and personal loan. It gives accurate EMI results instantly using a mathematical formula.

How EMI is Calculated?

EMI is calculated using formula:
EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]
Where P = Loan amount, R = monthly interest rate, N = number of months.
